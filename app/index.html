This project is a database to support the Homeless Coordinated Entry program in Albany County, New York. “Coordinated entry is a process developed to ensure that all people experiencing a housing crisis have fair and equal access and are quickly identified, assessed for, referred, and connected to housing and assistance based on their strengths and needs.” Coordinated entry is a federal mandate from the Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD) and is a fairly new system. Because of this, there is a lack of software infrastructure to support this program. There has been a wealth of data collected pertaining to coordinated entry, and perhaps this database could potentially help organize this data and serve in a real world application.
